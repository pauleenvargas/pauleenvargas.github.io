Introduction

What is cyber bullying?

Cyberbullying is a form of bullying through gadgets. It is also called online bullying. It happens mostly on social media sites, and mostly on teenagers, online stars and such.

'Buddies, not Bullies' workshop tends to give information about cyberbullying. Also informs you how to prevent & stop cyberbullying. How to help those who are experiencing cyberbullying. This workshop can help you know a lot about cyberbullying and how you can help yourself.

OBJECTIVES:

At the end of our presentation, we are aiming to:

1. Our goal for our workshop is to spread awareness on how to help someone whenever they’re facing cyberbullying.
2. We would share hints for them to know when a friend/family member is facing cyberbullying.
3. We also want to make sure they will feel better after our activities, sessions, and many more.

Expectations From the Workshop

In our workshop, participants will be able to do quizzes, discussion and Q&A’s. We have three sessions.

Session 1

In this session our team explained what is cyberbullying and how it can affect us.

Session 2

You will learn how we know if we or someone is getting bullied in this session.

Session 3

Lastly, in session three we will have ways to prevent and stop cyberbullying.

Open Forum & Evaluation

Before the workshop ends, our team will make the participants answer a short survey for feedback.
